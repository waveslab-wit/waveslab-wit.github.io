Our Mission

The WAVES Laboratory advances research in wireless artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and digital twins. We focus on designing resilient intelligent networks that can operate securely in dynamic and adversarial environments.

What We Do

Our lab explores challenges in spectrum-driven AI, wireless resilience, and secure communication systems.

Opportunities for Students

Co-Op and undergraduate research opportunities give students hands-on experience in next-generation network technologies.

Future Outlook

We aim to establish a cutting-edge wireless AI testbed, enabling impactful research and sponsored innovation.
